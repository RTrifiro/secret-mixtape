The Process

How it works

Simple by design. Mysterious by nature.

01
Sign up as a Listener

Give us your name, address, and email. Answer three questions — pulled at random just for you. About a mood, a memory, a moment. Then wait.

02
A Maker gets your answers

A Maker is matched with you, randomly. They’ll see your three answers — and nothing else. That’s their brief.

03
They make the tape

A real cassette or CD. Curated track by track, for you specifically. They cover postage. They seal it up. They send it.

04
It arrives in your mailbox

Press play. Someone, somewhere, thought about you while making this. That’s the whole thing.

About

Why physical?
Why strangers?

Streaming is endless. Playlists are disposable. A mixtape — a real one, something you can hold — is a commitment. Someone sat with a blank tape and made choices. Every song is a decision.

And strangers, it turns out, can surprise you. They’re not trying to impress you or manage your opinion of them. They’re just listening to what you said, and responding with music.

Secret Mixtape is free. No subscriptions. Makers cover their own costs because they want to — because making a mixtape for a stranger is its own reward. We just make the introduction.
